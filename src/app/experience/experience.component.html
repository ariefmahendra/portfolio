Mirror and Stationary Cycle (Mirocle)

Github Youtube

Mirocle is an innovative therapeutic product that specifically designed to help post stroke patients in restoring their motor function. By combining concept of mirror therapy and stationary bike, Mirocle provides a stimulus that is to the brain and the patient's nervous system.

In this project, my team and I designed the Mirocle project with the features that are: Monitoring heart rate, oxygen saturation, calories burned, number of pedal revolutions and duration. Monitoring is IoT. Monitoring is done through the website. The protocol used for communication is MQTT with QoS level 0. data can be downloaded in csv format
